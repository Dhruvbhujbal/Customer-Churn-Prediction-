About This Dashboard

Welcome to the Customer Retention Engine. This tool allows account managers to predict the likelihood of a customer canceling their service in real-time.

How it Works

This system is powered by a Random Forest Machine Learning Algorithm trained on thousands of historical customer records. It analyzes billing habits, contract types, and service usage to identify hidden flight risks.

Instructions

1. Enter the customer's current details into the portal.
2. Click "Run Prediction Engine".
3. If the risk is high, route the customer to the retention team immediately.
